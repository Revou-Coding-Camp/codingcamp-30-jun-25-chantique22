About Holland Bakery

Holland Bakery is Indonesia leading bakery chains with 25 branches (Central Kitchen) that manage more than 470 outlets: Jabodetabek (Gajah Mada, Pondok Pinang, Jatinegara, Cikini, Sunter, Serpong, Ciputat, Pekayon, Bogor and Karawang), Bandung , Surabaya, Lampung, Batam, Pekanbaru, Makassar, Manado, Bali, Solo, Semarang, Balikpapan and Samarinda. We are still continuously expanding nationwide to other cities.


Holland Bakery is one of the first pioneer in modern bakery business in Indonesia. Established in 1978, Holland Bakery is currently managed under PT. Mustika Citra Rasa. Our products are healthy, nutritious and affordable by everyone.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To make Holland Bakery’s products a preferred staple food choice for the Indonesian people.

Our Mission

  • Develop high-quality, nutritious products suited to Indonesian tastes.
  • Expand our store network across the country.
  • Enhance employee welfare and service excellence.

Our Profile

Holland Bakery is a well-known bread brand in Indonesia with more than 40 years of experience. Founded in 1978, we have become a pioneer of modern bread and continue to grow in various major cities in Indonesia.

We provide more than 200 types of products, including sweet bread, white bread, tarts, cookies, and traditional cakes. Each product is processed with modern technology and high-quality ingredients.

With MUI halal certification and commitment to quality, we are determined to continue to be the main choice of Indonesian people for bread and cake products.
